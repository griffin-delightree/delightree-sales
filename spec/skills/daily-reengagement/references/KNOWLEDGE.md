# Delightree Prospecting Knowledge Base
_Compiled from Justin's Google Drive (sales enablement) on 2026-06-18. Sources cited inline._

## 1. ICP FIT
Delightree = operations/training/SOP/task/audit/compliance/comms platform for FRANCHISE & MULTI-LOCATION brands ("AI-Powered Franchisor Super App"). Target = franchisors & multi-unit operators, NOT single units. Buyer may be the franchisor HQ or a large multi-unit franchisee.
Size bands (Building Target Prospect Lists): 10-25 (founder-led), 26-50 (mid), 50+ (large, dedicated teams). Minimum viable ~10 locations. Build 4-7 prospects/company (5-6 min).
Disqualifiers: single-location, corporate-only with no multi-unit scaling pain, or no findable decision-makers.

## 2. TIERING (High=T1 / Moderate=T2 / Low=T3, by size) - Building Target Prospect Lists
- 10-25: T1 = CEO/Founder, Operations. T2 = Training, Support, FBCs. T3 = Franchise Dev, HR, Finance.
- 26-50: T1 = Operations, Support, Compliance, then CEO/Founder. T2 = Training, FBCs, District/Regional VPs, Growth, CSO/CGO/CTO. T3 = Franchise Dev, HR, Finance.
- 50+: T1 = Operations, Support, Compliance, PLUS any C-Suite/Founder/President (always T1). T2 = Training, Finance, FBCs, District/Regional VPs, Growth. T3 = Franchise Dev, HR, Finance.
Franchise Dev / HR / Finance are ALWAYS Low (T3).
> C-SUITE OVERRIDE (per Justin 2026-06-26): any C-level executive, Founder, or President is ALWAYS Tier 1 in EVERY band. This supersedes any '50+ CEO drops off T1' note above.
 Fallback if ICP roles absent: Director of Finance (ROI/AUV angle), then FBCs/Area Developers.

## 3. VALUE PROPS / OUTCOMES
One platform for SOPs/playbook, training/LMS, task mgmt, audits/compliance, comms, AI knowledge base, new-location openings. Sell: location-level VISIBILITY, higher audit scores, higher task/training completion, faster onboarding & openings, less HQ support burden, franchisee engagement, consistent execution at scale "without adding corporate headcount." Delightree DOES replace point tools (do not say "we won't replace your stack").

## 5. EMAIL RULES
> RESEARCH-BACKED POSITIONING (per Justin 2026-06-26): before writing, pull from RECENT (2025) studies and leading-org sources (IFA, FRANdata, Franchise Business Review, Black Box Intelligence, Cornell, Gong, reputable franchise trade press) to ground the insight and the stat. One verifiable 2025 third-party stat per email; never invent. - Delightree Email Playbook
Length 90-130 words, mobile-friendly. 6-part anatomy: (1) opener = their words or a hard sourced fact (NO compliment, NO "I noticed"); (2) insight reframe; (3) gap evidence; (4) ONE verifiable third-party stat (IFA, FRANdata, Black Box, Cornell, Gong); (5) "dead ends" = two obvious fixes that fail, do NOT name Delightree here; (6) CTA = "Delightree" appears only in the CTA.
Subjects: 3 options, lowercase, <=33 chars, punny when it fits. CTA by role: C-suite = intelligent interest question (no calendar ask); Dir/VP = interest CTA + named-customer proof; Manager = slightly more direct; warm/deal-stage = specific time ask.
Voice: T1 default professional (no contractions) unless socials read casual; T2 match their voice.
HARD: NO em dashes anywhere (use commas or periods instead) - non-negotiable; every stat sourced (never invent); named customers only when real; bold numbers; max one ":)"; soft CTA. NO AI tells (no negative parallelism "not X, it's Y", no rule-of-three, no hollow openers, no "hope you're doing well", no filler transitions).

## 6. CALL / VOICEMAIL - Sales Prospecting & Voicemail Playbook
3-touch VM cadence: VM1 (Day 1) <15s, first name only, no company/pitch. VM2 (Day 3-5) name+company, reference the email, say email address. VM3 (Day 7-10) name+company/role + a specific milestone/stat + drop a customer name. Cold-call open: "Hi [Name], Justin from Delightree... I'll keep this quick," then problem-first pitch + probing question + soft meeting ask.

## 7. PERSONAS (angle that resonates)
- Operations (primary champion/decision-maker): consistency, visibility, task mgmt, faster onboarding. Pain: inconsistent execution, missed tasks, manual compliance, no real-time data.
- Training: onboarding speed, completion rates, standardized engaging mobile training; verifiable "sticks."
- Franchise Support: franchisee satisfaction, centralized comms, less HQ load, automated audits.
- Franchise Development (T3): faster launches, pre-opening tracking.
- VP/Dir Franchise Operations: economic+operational decision-maker; triggers = rapid growth, low visibility, inconsistent execution, franchisees over-relying on HQ.
- FBC (influencer + end-user): pre-visit visibility, automated audits, coach with real-time data instead of chasing.

## 8. OBJECTIONS / POSTURE
"Already have a solution" -> reframe gaps. "Just send info" -> convert to 15-20 min tailored convo. "No budget" -> reframe as priority/value. "Not a priority" -> future-seed. "Bad timing" -> lock next step. "Wrong person" -> ask for owner + intro. "Too expensive" -> tie to labor saved/audit misses. Competitor lock-in -> fit without ripping out; offer 1-2 store pilot. Position as the ONLY unified playbook+task+audit+AI platform built for franchise scale. Always research first (LinkedIn, news, podcasts, 2025 FDD) and end each account with an ADR->AE handoff.
