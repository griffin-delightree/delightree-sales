## 0. THE BAR
Every contact handed to Justin must be (a) a CURRENT employee, re-verified THIS run, (b) clear of all guardrails below, and (c) accompanied by real prior context. If sources conflict, HOLD OUT and flag. Never invent a person, title, meeting, quote, or stat.

## 1. ACTIVE-EMPLOYMENT VERIFICATION (run for every contact, every run - even carryovers)
- THREE sources: (1) LIVE LinkedIn in the connected Chrome browser = DECISIVE; (2) ZoomInfo employmentHistory (corroborate, never override); (3) company About/Team page (context + leadership/title/location changes).
- NOT active if LinkedIn shows: a different current employer, the brand only under past experience, an end-date on the role, or a franchise-sales/brokerage firm (FOC, FranServe, FranChoice, IFPG). A name on a company team page is NOT proof of employment (brands list outsourced brokers as "team").
- LINKEDIN UNAVAILABLE (auth wall / browser down): fall back to ZoomInfo - accept "active" ONLY if validated within ~90 days, and add the note "LinkedIn not checked this run."
- #OpenToWork while still listing the company as current -> INCLUDE with a caution flag (do not silently treat as clean-active).
- SAME-PERSON check (employment history / location / role lineage) before trusting any same-name match.
- LOG every check (VERIFIED ACTIVE + sources + date / DEPARTED / HELD OUT) in CONTACT-VERIFICATION-LOG.md; read it before every run and never re-surface a departed/held-out contact as active.

## 2. PARENT / AE-OVERLAP / ACTIVE-DEAL GUARDRAILS (check FIRST, before any research effort)
- PARENT / HOLDING: flag the parent first thing. If an AE is working the parent brand -> DO NOT prospect. If research shows no clear decision-making structure -> flag who the parent is and move on. List any parent-company contacts under a separate "Parent Company" section.
- PARENT TOUCH TEST: if the parent has had a meeting in the last 30 days, OR a future meeting scheduled, OR an open deal -> do NOT prospect the child brand. If the parent is untouched with no future tasks -> the child brand is fair game.
- ACTIVE-DEAL / BEING-WORKED (the company itself): ONLY an OPEN DEAL or a FUTURE-DATED MEETING blocks prospecting. Active sequences, recent replies, or a PAST meeting do NOT block. A past meeting with no open deal and no future meeting = prospect as a WARM RECONNECT.
- FAILURE -> DROP and REPLACE with the next eligible company (or, for a single-account request, STOP and tell Justin why).

## 3. PRIOR CONTEXT & WARM/FRESH GATE (look back 24 months)
- Pull and summarize: deals (incl. CLOSED-LOST: stage, amount, close date, lost reason) + notes + meetings + logged calls/replies - enough to detect any real prospect response.
- WARM RECONNECT only if the PROSPECT actually engaged us (a reply, a logged conversation, a note of what they said, or a meeting). Unreplied one-way outbound = FRESH. Never imply a relationship that did not happen.
- CLOSED-LOST -> reference the prior deal + lost reason and lead with WHAT HAS CHANGED on our end.
- FOLLOW-UP ASK: if anyone asked us to reach back out (a date/condition), flag it at the TOP of the account (who, when, context) and prioritize timing accordingly.

## 4. DATA RECENCY
- 2025 data, quotes, and facts ONLY. Default third-party stat = IFA 2025 Franchising Economic Outlook (~851,000 U.S. units; +~20,000 units; +~210,000 jobs; output >$936.4B). Any other stat (FRANdata/Black Box/Cornell/Gong) must be 2025 or be dropped. Location counts, leadership, and funding must reflect verified 2025 reality.

## 5. DRAFTS ONLY
- Never enroll, send, or schedule anything. Produce drafts; Justin enrolls/sends.

## 6. COMPLIANCE
- B2B research only; honor ZoomInfo ToS and applicable privacy law. Capture HQ line; flag HQ/shared lines (not direct dials). Signature on all copy: "Best, Justin Hoke | Book a Meeting | Delightree".
